About Football (Soccer)

Football, known as soccer in some countries, is a globally beloved team sport played by two teams of eleven players. Over more than a century the game has grown from local competitions to worldwide tournaments, inspiring generations of players and fans. This site highlights some of the game's greatest players — how they shaped clubs, won trophies, and left legacies from the early days of organized football to the modern era.
